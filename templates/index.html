About Expense Splitter

Expense Splitter is a platform designed to help you and your friends effortlessly keep track of shared expenses during trips, events, or any other occasions.
Have you ever returned from an evening with friends and realized that you bought a round of drinks out of turn? Or perhaps struggled to remember who paid for dinner or the hotel during a group vacation? Expense Splitter is here to save the day!

Our easy-to-use interface allows you to register, login, create or join groups to document and split expenses with friends, making the process of settling debts a breeze.
Say goodbye to awkward conversations and manually calculating who owes whom. With Expense Splitter, you can keep your friendships intact and your finances organized.

Whether you're planning a weekend getaway, a dinner party, or just hanging out with friends, Expense Splitter ensures that everyone pays their fair share without any confusion or misunderstandings.
Experience the convenience and peace of mind that comes with using Expense Splitter for all your group expenses.
